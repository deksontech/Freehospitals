Terms

Using Freehospitals

Freehospitals helps patients discover doctors and submit contact requests. The website does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency care, or guaranteed appointments.

Doctor Listings

Doctor information is maintained from available profile details and admin updates. Patients should confirm availability, fees, hospital rules, and medical suitability before visiting.

Free Contact Requests

Submitting a request on Freehospitals is free of cost. Any separate consultation, hospital, diagnostic, or treatment charges are handled directly by the relevant provider.

Emergency Care

For medical emergencies, call local emergency services or visit the nearest hospital immediately.
