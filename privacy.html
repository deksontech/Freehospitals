Privacy Policy

How Freehospitals Handles Patient Information

Freehospitals collects the details you submit in contact forms, including name, phone, email, city, preferred contact time, and health concern, so the care desk can help connect you with a doctor.

Information We Use

We use submitted information to respond to requests, coordinate follow-ups, improve doctor discovery, and maintain internal records for support quality.

Sharing

We may share request details with the care desk and relevant doctor, clinic, or hospital teams for coordination. We do not sell patient contact information.

Emergency Care

Freehospitals is not an emergency medical service. For medical emergencies, call local emergency services or visit the nearest hospital immediately.
